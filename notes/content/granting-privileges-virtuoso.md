Title: Granting SPARQL privileges with Virtuoso
Date: 2014-03-25
Category: debug
Tags: info, triple-store
Author: Vincent

Launch the virtuoso server.

Go to the [conductor](http://localhost:8890/conductor/), then:

  1. System Admin

  2. User Accounts

  3. SPARQL, click edit.

  4. Account roles, put `SPARQL_*` in the Selected field.
